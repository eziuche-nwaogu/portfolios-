I'm a Backend Developer and Data Engineer based in Nigeria with over a decade of experience spanning enterprise IT infrastructure, automation, and software engineering. My journey began in hardware — maintaining IBM and Lenovo BladeCenter servers for MTN Nigeria — and evolved into architecting scalable software solutions.

Today I specialize in building robust ETL pipelines with Apache Airflow, crafting RESTful APIs with Django and FastAPI, and designing database architectures with PostgreSQL and MySQL. I also teach data engineering and backend development at LearnFactory Nigeria.

My vision: "To deliver high-impact digital solutions that drive innovation, automate workflows, and empower people through knowledge and technology."
